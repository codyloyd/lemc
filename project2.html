Project 2

Compose a song within the following guidelines.

    project guidelines:

  1. Feel completely free to add as many other tracks as needed, but your song should at least have these 7 tracks:
    1. Kick Drum
    2. Snare Drum
    3. Hi Hat
    4. Melody
    5. Secondary Melody
    6. Chords
    7. Bass
  2. The 2 melody tracks do not need to play at the same time... they can be used in different parts of the song.
  3. your song should contain 2 main sections melodies, each of which is 64 or 128 lines long (the sections can be 1 or 2 patterns long)
  4. YOU select the tempo
  5. use effects to enhance the sound.
  6. the drum beat is up to you, but make sure it is cohesive by placing snare hits on beats 2 and 4
  7. arrange your song so that it loops between the sections nicely.. i.e. feel free to repeat the main sections a time or two so that it's not switching between the sections too quickly.
  8. the 2 sections should be noticibly contrasting, so do any (or all) of the following to distinguish them:
    • change drum beat
    • change melody
    • change the instruments
    • change the length of the pattern
    • change the chords
  9. the sections MUST be in the same key.. but do not have to use the same chords.
  10. arrange your two sections into a song by AT LEAST going back to the first section after playing the second.
  11. feel free to add variations to the sectons when they repeat.
  12. feel free to create a little "intro" and "outro" for your song.
