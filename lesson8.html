Lesson 8 - FORM

We've already talked quite a bit about form in class, so this lesson doesn't have much content beyond a simple reminder of what I've already covered.

Form is simply the term for the way that composers manipulate and use the various different melodies and sections in their music. Of course, the topic CAN get quite complex, but typically, most popular music (i.e. the stuff we're trying to write) is relatively formally simple.

However, despite the fact that form is a relatively simple concept, it is hugely important because no one really wants to listen to 3 or 4 minutes of the EXACT same thing looping over and over again. Sometimes, however it can be kind of tricky to actually implement successfully in your music... so we practice...
