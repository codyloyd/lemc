Assignment 8 - Form

For this assignment, you will email me your completed project file.

    You're going to write me 2 simple song outlines that follow the guidelines below:

  1. Write for 2 instruments only:
    • a piano/keyboard type instrument for playing chords
    • any other instrument for playing the melody (can be another keyboard or piano)
  2. keep the chord instrument simple (i.e. just play each chord once and let it ring.. don't do fancy rhythms or arpeggiations)
  3. each section should be 32 lines long (go ahead and change the pattern length in renoise so that each section of the form is 1 pattern)... and each song will be 4 patterns
  4. the form of the first outline should be a a b a
  5. the form of the second should be a b a c
  6. put each song in a different key
  7. if you must.. you may drop a drum loop in for each song, but don't waste time trying to create the perfect beat, that's not the focus of this assignment.
