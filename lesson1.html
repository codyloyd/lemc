Lesson 1 - Using Renoise

This section of the course based on writing and composing electronic music is going to cover a variety of topics, but for the most part, it is all going to center around using the program Renoise

The program itself is not free, but it's demo version is (almost) fully featured. The demo is what we will be using for this class. I will show you how to find a copy of the demo that you will drag onto the Desktop of whatever computer you are using. Feel free to copy the demo onto a USB flash drive if you like.

Your First Assignment is to watch this video, follow along, and create a simple drum beat (like the one in the vid). The video will be easier to follow if you open it up in a new tab and watch it full-screen.

When you've finished with your own short drum-beat, let me listen to it.
