Assignment 9 - Mixing

For this assignment, you will email me your completed project file.

    Open up the provided file in Renoise:

    FILE
  1. Do your best to mix this song.. I've intentionally made it a little bit messy (i.e. there's too much going on) to give you a bit of a challenge
  2. Group related tracks together (feel free to move tracks around) and mix them separately before trying to mix the whole song.
  3. Make sure that no tracks are clipping
  4. USE A COMPRESSOR. You don't need it on every track, but use one at least 4 times. Hint: you might want to put a compressor on an individial track, AND the group that the track belongs to. AAAAAND maybe the master as well.
  5. Use as many other effects as you feel you need (EQ/REVERB/FILTER/etc.)
