If you've completed the previous two assignments you should already have a song begun with the following: A selection of instruments and a form framework. Essentially all you have to do now is fill it out!

In the previous assignment you should have more or less copied the form of a different song. This does not mean that you should try to copy the actual notes and patterns of the song. You should, however consider the way the sections work together... does the song change intensity between sections at all? Is one of the sections a "build-up"? questions like these can be used to formulate an idea of what your song is supposed to do.

I don't want to be too perscriptive with how you approach your song. I don't care if you start with the drums, or the chords or whatever. I will say, though that you'll find it easiest to start with one of the main sections (i.e. not the intro) and try to at least get a good sketch of the entire section before moving on.

This is a long and involved project, so I expect it will take quite a bit of time to complete. Try not to get bogged down on small details until the whole song form is filled out.
