Lesson 10 - PLUGINS

Not a ton of new info here... just a part of the program that you haven't really used before.

I've shown you how to use plugins in class so all you need to do is get them loaded up and then play around with them a bit.

To load the available plugins go to the public 'Userfiles/High School/loyd/musicapp/' folder and copy the entire 'vst' folder to your desktop. (you can just click and drag it over.)

In Renoise, press ctl+, (the control key and the comma key at the same time) to open up the 'Preferences' menu. In that window the plugins are the last option on the list: "Plug/Misc"

At the top of that page under VST Plugins there will be a "Browse" button.. click it and then find the VST folder you put on your desktop. (then click OK". That should take a few moments to load.. but when it has you should be good to go. You can find the plugins on the "plugin" tab like I showed you in class.

Some of the plugins are easier to use than others... and some are generally a little nicer than others, so take a few minutes to play around with each of them... at least figure out how to load different presets.
