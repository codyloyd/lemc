Assignment 4 - Basic Audio Effects

For this assignment, you will email me your completed project file.

  1. You are going to write 4 single pattern "songs" with drums (kick,hat,snare) and at least 1 melodic voice.
  2. Song 1: write in the Key of E minor and make sure to use delay as an effect on the melodic track.
  3. Song 2: write in the Key of F major and use Reverb as an effect on at least one of the tracks.
  4. Song 3: write in the Key of A minor and use Distortion.
  5. Song 4: write in any key and use a combination of effects on any of the tracks.
