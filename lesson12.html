Lesson 12 - EXPRESSSSSIVITITY

Most people agree that emotionally expressive music is the best music. HOWEVER: in many cases electronic music can turn out to be rather dry and bland because of the rigid programmed nature of it. This lesson is going to discuss a few techniques for making your electronic music a little more expressive.

Main Idea => make stuff move!

In most cases, evolving sounds are going to be judged as more expressive than static sounds so these techniques are going to be based upon making sounds that change over time.

Velocity

In the realm of music production, velocity is the volume and intensity of a note. It is simply a setting that can be manipulated in Renoise for every note. The effect that changing velocity has on a note depends upon how the instrument is set up... some instruments are very sensitive to velocity, while others are less so.

Watch this video to see an example of sounds changing via velocity, and how to do it in renoise.

AUTOMATION
